About us

ABOUT OUR COMPANY

WE PROVIDE BEST SELECTIONS OF FINISHING MATERIALS AND CONSTRUCTION CHEMICALS

EKT CERAMICS® is a registered trade mark for The Klinkers Trading plc. The company has 18+ years of good reputation for delivering quality construction material solutions known with our mother company EKT Trade & Investment Plc.
Our mother company EKT Trade and investment is one of the pioneers in import & export business in Ethiopia. Since its establishment it has got solid experience in importing & distributing high-quality building finishing materials, and construction Chemicals. Furthermore, the company has manufacturing wing of plastic products. Because of the expansion and to serve our customers smoothly and on time we decided to separate an entity called The Klinkers Trading Plc with the existing registered trade mark EKT CERAMICS®.
The company has registered remarkable achievements by playing a significant role in the construction sector including different mega projects, a well-organized management structure, scientific marketing strategies and strong business network established with different partners can be considered as the core value of EKT CERAMICS®️.
Throughout our experience in the business sector, it has been the priority of our company to engage in the supply of quality and modern materials to the construction industry.
